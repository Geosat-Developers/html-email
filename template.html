Our Highlight: BlueTraker VMS

BlueTraker VMS is designed for long-term durability and compliance. It supports encrypted data transmission, remote configuration, and environmental sensors, giving authorities and fleet managers deep insight into operations.

  • Fully autonomous operation with built-in battery backup
  • Supports multiple satellite systems for redundancy and global reach
  • Integrated GNSS, GSM, and Iridium transceivers for full situational awareness
  • Web-based dashboard for real-time monitoring and reporting

Our Highlight: SmartOne Solar

In addition to the BlueTraker VMS, we offer the SmartOne Solar, a cutting-edge vessel monitoring system designed for smaller vessels and harsh marine environments. This device ensures reliable tracking and communication, even in the most remote locations.

  • Powered by solar energy for sustainable operation
  • IP69-rated for extreme weather resistance
  • GlobalStar satellite connectivity for worldwide coverage
  • Ideal for fishing boats, tugboats, and supply vessels

With its compact design and robust features, the SmartOne Solar is the perfect solution for vessels requiring efficient and eco-friendly monitoring systems.
